IN THE NEWS: MOULTON ON THE ISRAEL-HAMAS CONFLICT


**WASHINGTON, DC –** In the days since the start of the Israel Hamas conflict, Congressman Moulton has been outspoken about the horrific Hamas terrorist attack on Israel, and Israel's right to defend itself. At the same time, Congressman Moulton is urging Israel to heed the lessons America learned in the War on Terror.

In a recent [op-ed on CNN.com,](https://www.cnn.com/2023/10/17/opinions/israel-military-strategy-after-hamas-moulton/index.html) **Congressman Seth Moulton (D-MA)** argues that Israel must learn from America’s mistakes in Iraq and Afghanistan if it is to avoid a “forever war” and minimize even more loss of innocent life on both sides. Drawing from his experiences as a Marine conducting a counter-insurgency campaign in Najaf, Iraq, Moulton urges Israeli leaders to focus on establishing the “big ideas” — or the desired end game — and a viable path to getting there.
